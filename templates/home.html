How It Works

Lurora makes storytelling easy and interactive. Follow these three simple steps to create and share your own AI-powered story:
1
Create an Account:
Sign up and log in to access the Lurora platform. Your account allows you to manage and share your stories.
2
Generate a Story
Select a topic, and let the AI generate a five-scene narrative complete with visuals and transition questions to enhance engagement.
3
Encrypt and Share
Hide secret messages within your story and challenge others to uncover the hidden meanings. Share your creation with the Lurora community and explore stories made by others.
